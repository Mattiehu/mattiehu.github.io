Sunset eye

Sunset in Montauk

illustrator version

Original image

I chose this image because I am someone who absolutely loves capturing sunsets. This is my favorite sunset photo; I took it in Montauk, Long Island, during a road trip with my family in 2022. I used several different brushes and three colors—black, orange, and white—but I found it quite interesting that when I applied the white paint over large areas, it seemed to take on a slightly grayish tint. Overall, I found the brush tool to be extremely useful, and the ability to create distinct layers by using different colors was a tremendous help to me.
